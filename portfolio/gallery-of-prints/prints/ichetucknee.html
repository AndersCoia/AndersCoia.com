Ichetucknee Springs State Park - Blue Hole Poster

This is a print of a spring fed river in North Central Florida that I grew up going to, on the print you can see cypress trees in the background, really really clear water with manatee grass and a few gars, then a pair of ducks and great heron.

Print information

  • Obtained: Florida, 2021
  • Reson: It was a Christmas gift from my Dad so that my whole family would have this to hang up in their homes.

Relationship with Original

  • Date: 2008
  • Every single summer I would go kayaking on this river together with my Dad, The scene in the poster is something I've never quite seen because it is in Winter

Technical Information

  • Inscription: ICHETUCKNEE SPRINGS, STATE PARK, FLORIDA, BLUE HOLE
  • Material: Matte paper
  • Dimensions: 29 x 42 cm
