Cervélo 2022 Tour de France Poster

A picture of the 2022 Tour de France from a magazine I bought before watching the 2023 Tour in France, the poster reminds me of my trip to europe.

Print information

  • Obtained: France, 2023
  • Reson: It came in a magazine about the Tour de France that I purchased before waching the race in person in 2023.

Relationship with Original

  • Date: 2023
  • Though I didn't see the race depicted, I watched the 2023 Tour de France and the racers looked just as exhausted as they did in the picture.

Technical Information

  • Inscription: Wout van Aert leads Jonas Vingegaard to victory in stage 18 of the 2022 Tour de France.
  • Material: Gloss magazine paper.
  • Dimensions: 90 x 60 cm
