Group X, Altarpiece, no 3

Hilma af Klint

One of Hilma af Klints scene disrupting works, using geometric shapes to convey an abstract sense of spiritualism.

Print information

  • Reson: Because it was on sale and I had just recently learned about Hilma af Klint in class.
