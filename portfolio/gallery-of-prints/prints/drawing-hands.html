Drawing Hands

M.C. Escher

A detailed pencil drawing in which the positioning of the hands creates a sense of infinity.

Print information

  • Obtained: South Yarra, Melbourne, 1989
  • Reson: It spoke to me in a profound way

Relationship with Original

  • Date: 2018
  • Saw the print before I saw the original. The original is much smaller, and it was hard to see much of the detail that I had grown to love, though I still love the original work.

Technical Information

  • Inscription: E26 / M.C. ESCHER TEKENEN-DRAWING HANDS-ZEICHNEN-DESSINER, Published by Cordon Art P.O. Box 101, Baarn, Holland. Copyright 1988 M.C. Escher c/o Cordon Art. All rights reserved. Printed by Bakker Baarn Holland.
  • Material: Matte paper.
  • Dimensions: 56 x 65 cm.
